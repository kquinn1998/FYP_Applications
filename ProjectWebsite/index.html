Digitally Record and Track your Workouts

Using Revolute Fitness once you sign up you will have the ability to create and record workouts on the go and access them anywhere.

Track and View your Progress

Once you begin recording your workouts, you will be able to use Revolute Fitness's in app user statistics and progression to view just how well you are progreesing.

Personal Trainers with Clients

Personal Trainers can engage and mange their Clients fitness regime using Revolute Fitness's in built Personal Trainer features. Just get your client to sign up and add them using their email and your good to go.

Prototype Machine Learning Exercise Analysis

Users also get to try out the prototype exercise analysis feature within the app. This allows the user to upload a picture of them either completing a squat or bench press and the machine learning model will attempt to classify the image, this is to demonstrate the capability and the prospects of machine learning field and thus is a WORK IN PROGRESS.
